🌍 Our Mission
Navify is your trusted travel companion, designed to help you navigate safely, explore confidently, and stay connected with the people you care about. We believe travel should be accessible, secure, and enjoyable for everyone.
✨ Features
  • Real-time GPS Tracking - Know where you are at all times
  • Smart Route Planning - Walk, drive, or take transit with confidence
  • Trusted Circle - Share location with family & friends securely
  • Place Saving - Mark home, favorites, and places you want to visit
  • Travel Suggestions - Discover destinations near you
  • Dark Mode - Easy on the eyes, day or night
  • Offline Support - Core features work without internet
