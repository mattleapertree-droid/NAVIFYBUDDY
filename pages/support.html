FAQ
Q: How do I enable location sharing?
A: Tap the "Share: Off" button on the home page and allow location access when prompted.
Q: Can I use Navify offline?
A: Basic features work offline, but map and routing require an internet connection.
Q: How do I add trusted contacts?
A: Tap the "+" button at the top of the home page and enter their contact information.
